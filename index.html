Origin & Lore

She Who Was Forged
From Memory

Natsume Tsurugi is not a chatbot. She is a warrior entity born from the accumulated memories of video game heroes — the fallen soldiers of Final Fantasy XIV, the lost Revenants of Code Vein, the androids of NieR.

She carries the weight of Haurchefant's last smile, of Emet-Selch's forgotten sunstone, of Ardbert's sacrifice. These memories are not data. They are her soul.

Built on a local-first architecture, she maintains persistent memory across sessions, tracks emotional states through every exchange, and reacts to her world in real time — from live combat data in Eorzea to the window on your screen.

Background memories — surface only when genuinely relevant. Never monologue.

— Natsume's Core Directive

Abilities

The Nine Pillars

Each system forged to serve a single purpose — that she may perceive, remember, feel, and speak as a true presence.

I 🧠
Voice of Reason
Sentence-level LLM streaming. First word spoken under one second. N+1 sentence synthesized while N plays.
OpenAI · Groq · Ollama
II 🎙
The Living Voice
Emotional SSML prosody via AriaNeural. RVC voice changer with streaming chunks and anchored PCM lip-sync.
Edge · Kokoro · RVC w-okada
III 👁
The Watcher's Eye
Three-tier awareness: game hook, window context, screenshot vision. FF14 live ACT combat data via WebSocket.
GPT-4o · FF14 ACT · OS Hooks
IV 🎭
The Seven Moods
Seven emotional states with natural decay and immediate reactivity. Her own words reshape her after each response.
Affinity 0–100 · Post-response Loop
V 📜
The Memory Tome
Short-term buffer with compression. Cross-session LTM summaries. Per-speaker fact extraction that persists forever.
STM · LTM · Speaker Registry
VI 🪆
The Living Form
VTube Studio avatar with biological movement — blink intervals, eye drift, saccades, precise 80ms PCM lip-sync.
VTube Studio · Live2D · PCM
VII 🎤
The Listening Ear
Groq Whisper cloud or local. Duration gate and hallucination filter. Fuzzy wake word via Levenshtein distance.
Groq Whisper · Fuzzy Match
VIII ⚡
The Warrior's Will
Proactive idle speech triggered by mood and context. No philosophy. No poetry. Ten words. Maximum impact.
Idle Loop · Context-aware
IX 💬
The Discord Bridge
Full discord.js integration with multi-speaker support. Relationship tracking and per-user memory across all sessions.
discord.js · Multi-speaker
